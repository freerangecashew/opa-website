kobuk valley

kobuk valley is Alaska's most preserved National Park. Famous for Kobuk River and the largest sand dunes in the Arctic. Kobuk River is renowned for world class fishing.

national historic landmark

onion portage

Onion Portage archeological site is a national historic landmark named after the wild onions that grow along the riverbank. Thousands of caribou migrate through Onion Portage each year.

ambler

Your trip beigins here! Ambler is a native village filled with heritage. With a population under 300 the village is community driven and full of cultural traditions.
