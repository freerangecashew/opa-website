the experience

Here's a preview of what to expect on your adventure to the arctic with onion portage adventures.

the flight

You’ll fly to O.P.A. via bush plane, while viewing Alaskan nature from above the clouds.

arriving in ambler

Your guide will meet you at Ambler’s airport to drive you to your arctic camp. Walking after travelling isn’t so fun.

the campsite

Finally you can unpack and relax in your cabin or arctic oven tent. Many of our activities can be done from just outside your door.

activities

Choose the activities that'll make your adventure unforgettable. O.P.A. can provide as much or little gear and assistance as desired so your adventure is truly your own.

down time

Between planned activities feel free to grab a fishing pole, read your favorite book, cook by the warm campire, and spot animals all in the natural beauty of the Alaskan wilderness.
